## 2
$$
\begin{align}
A(AB - BA) = (AB - BA)A \\
A^2B - ABA = ABA - BA^2 \\
2ABA = A^2B + BA^2
\\\\
B(AB - BA) = (AB - BA)B\\
BAB - B^2A = AB^2 - BAB\\
2BAB = AB^2 + B^2A
\\\\
2(ABA - BAB) = A^2B + BA^2 - AB^2 - B^2A
\end{align}
